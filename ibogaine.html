Deep in the rainforests of Central Africa grows Tabernanthe iboga — an unassuming shrub with orange fruits. The Bwiti tradition of Gabon and Cameroon has used the root bark in initiation rituals for centuries. A full iboga ceremony lasts 24 to 36 hours — the longest known psychedelic experience.
