Researchers at Johns Hopkins and the University of Maastricht call 5-MeO-DMT the most reliable inducer of complete mystical experiences — scoring higher than psilocybin, LSD or ayahuasca on the Mystical Experience Questionnaire. 80% of participants report an experience they describe as "more real than reality."
